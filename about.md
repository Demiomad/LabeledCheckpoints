# Labeled Checkpoints
This mod adds a <cy>percentage label</c> to practice checkpoints.

## Who is this for?
Anyone who forgot what percentage they placed a checkpoint in.

**If you've never had this problem, then this mod is probably <cr>not</c> for you.**

## Note
This mod does <cr>not</c> work on Platformer mode due to it not having a <cy>percentage</c>.
